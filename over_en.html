Olphaert den Otter (Poortugaal, 1955) studied at the Willem de Kooning Academie (1976 -
1981). He works with egg tempera, often in large series. One of these, de Refuge Morphology series
consisting of 127 works, was exhibited in 2008 in Museum Boijmans Van Beuningen. From time to time, Den Otter creates murals in pastel on location (Malta Contemporary Art and the KetelFactory, Schiedam) and animations. Alongside his activities as visual artist, he also sings countertenor in the Retorisch Kwartet and frequently lectures on cultural and philosophical themes.
